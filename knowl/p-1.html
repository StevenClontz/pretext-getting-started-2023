PreTeXt 1  is a document authoring system that allows you to convert the source of your document into a variety of output formats, including fully accessible webpages, PDF, Epub, Jupyter Notebooks, and braille. This “write once, read anywhere” approach has made it a popular choice for authors of Open Educational Resources, but PreTeXt can also be used to create other kinds of mathematical documents as well. Recent updates make this process much easier; there has never been a better time to get started with PreTeXt.
